Important Notes

  • RFBA impact: A Reportable Fringe Benefit Amount of approximately — will appear on your income statement, even though no FBT is paid. This may affect Medicare Levy Surcharge, HECS/HELP repayments, Family Tax Benefit, and child support calculations.
  • Residual value: This assumes the car is sold for exactly the ATO residual value at lease end. If it sells for more, the lease result improves. If less, it worsens.
  • Post-March 2027: The full FBT exemption may change (proposed but not yet legislated). Leases commencing before any rule change are expected to be grandfathered.
  • LCT eligibility: The car must be a BEV under the LCT threshold ($91,387 for 2026–27), first held and used on/after 1 July 2022, and must never have had LCT paid on it.
  • Salary below ~$45,000: The 21% marginal rate means savings are limited and the breakeven fee will be low.

This is a general estimate only. Individual results depend on specific lease terms, employer arrangements, and personal tax circumstances. Consult a financial adviser or tax professional before entering into a novated lease.
